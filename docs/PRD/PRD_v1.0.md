# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# HealthTwin AI X

Version: 1.0

Author: Agraj Nethra

Date: July 2026

---

# 1. Executive Summary

HealthTwin AI X is an AI-powered Digital Health Twin Platform designed to help individuals securely manage health information, understand medical reports, monitor wellness metrics, and receive AI-assisted health insights.

The platform combines modern web technologies, artificial intelligence, analytics, and secure health data management into a single ecosystem.

---

# 2. Vision

To empower individuals with a personalized digital health companion that promotes preventive healthcare through secure data management and explainable AI.

---

# 3. Mission

Develop a secure and scalable healthcare platform that allows users to:

- Store health records
- Upload medical reports
- Receive AI-generated report explanations
- Track medications
- Monitor health trends
- Manage appointments
- View analytics
- Share information securely with healthcare providers

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Healthcare information is fragmented across multiple applications.

Users often struggle to:

- Organize reports
- Track medications
- Understand medical terminology
- Monitor long-term health trends
- Share complete health history

---

# 5. Proposed Solution

HealthTwin AI X provides:

- Digital Health Twin
- AI Report Interpreter
- Health Dashboard
- Medication Management
- Health Analytics
- Secure Medical Vault
- Doctor Portal
- Admin Portal
